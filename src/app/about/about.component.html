He started to experiment with different genres and his travels led him to discover new things everyday and blah blah blah. He believes that his work should speak for him instead of a paragraph that he wrote in third person form. But, If you still wanna read, this is what you need to know : I'm no award winning, gallery featured, insta-famous photographer. I do it for myself. It's an in-expressible feeling to see my clicked shots on someone's wall giving them lifetime of memories. I make sure to connect to people on a personal level and I don't hurry any stuff. Good Quality requires Good amount of Time and I 100% agree to that. I take one shoot per day Max. Be it a 2 hour event or a whole 10 hour wedding. I think that's the reason I've never lost a client. You can count on my Punctuality, Attention to detail and Honesty to make sure that you're making a right decision. So, I guess I'll see you in the field and we'll talk more about ourselves :)
